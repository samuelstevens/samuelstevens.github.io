Papers I Like

In my third year of my PhD, I feel like a lot of papers I read are useless. Or a lot of ideas I have are useless. So I want to make a list of papers I do like and explain in 1-2 sentences why I like them, in an effort to understand what papers I find useful.

Great Papers

What do Vision Transformers Learn? A Visual Exploration

  • Compares CNNs and ViTs from an interpretability angle.
  • Leads to several insights about differences between ViTs vs CNNs and ViTs trained on classification vs CLIP.

Self-Supervised Learning from Images with a Joint-Embedding Predictive Architecture

  • Develops a new self-supervised training objective for vision models in the embedding space.

Good Papers

Vision Transformers Need Registers

  • Identifies a modeling issue and proposes a very simple fix with lots of analysis/ablations.
