Vision Transformers

The original vision transformer takes a standard encoder-only transformer (like BERT from NLP) and only modifies the embedding layer.

The embedding layer needs to change in two ways:

  1. The input is pixels, rather than tokens.
  2. “Tokens” are positioned in 2-dimensional space, rather than along a 1-dimensional line.
