Closing the Loop in Research

My thesis is probably going to be about developing AI for science, with a particular focus on ecology and organismal biology as the fields of science. Because of this, I think a lot about how to get my research into the hands of scientists, particularly those who are not familiar with AI, with programming, with software in general. That tends to lead to a very large improvement—if someone is already using ResNets to analyze data, switching to BioCLIP is an improvement, but not as big an improvement as getting BioCLIP in the hands of someone who analyzes data with their eyes and writes notes down. However, these users are also the least likely to use my research without any support from me.
